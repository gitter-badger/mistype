Mistype

Mistype is a webapp to help you and your users. How many times did you notice mistake or mistype on site and couldn't inform author, because you didn't know how?

But if author had integrated Mistype, you could just select mistyped text and hit Ctrl+Enter. That simple.

How to enable Mistype on your site? Include <script src="//mistype.co/js" data-typo data-owner="yourId"></script> and you're dnoe. Oh, did you notice mistype in the last sentence. Try it!
